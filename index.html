The History

Compelled by the undying passion for technology,Shorp entered the TV Segment in India with one purpose; offering unprecedented, groundbreaking technology with unmatched quality products and prices. With the tenets of the company grounded in quality and matchless technology, Shorp is steadfast in its adherence to push the company to unseen heights. With over 10 years of experience in TV industry, under the Group company ‘V. K. ELECTRONIC DEVICES PRIVATE LIMITED.’ Shorp aims to offer Right Quality Products at the Right Time & Right Price; refining the TV technology in India. Acclaimed as a brand for the Indian consumers,the brand has truly demonstrated quick and robust expansion since inception, showing productivity and growing sales since the first year of operations.

Having its manufacturing unit in Ghaziabad, Shorp has designed its varied product range suiting the needs and dynamic lifestyle of the audience. With focused attention to R&D, which is directed towards the needs of India and developing products for India,Shorp today has a robust portfolio of products with the latest affordable technology. Furthermore, Shorp also has many firsts to its credit for TV, which includes the first to support Indian power consumption patterns, Box Speakers, Air mouse and also the first to have the entire back end integration to manufacture TV, Shorp proudly Makes in India, Makes for India. Standing at the forefront of the technology, Shorp envisions to be suited in every Indian Home, at all times.
